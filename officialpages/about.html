Welcome to SafeSpace

1st Moroccan platform for mental well-being and support

What is SafeSpace?

SafeSpace is a virtual platform designed to address the growing mental health challenges among Moroccan high school students. By providing an intermediary between schools and psychologists, SafeSpace offers a supportive environment where students can engage in interactive activities aimed at reducing stress and anxiety. The platform is a tool for students to improve their mental health while fostering connections with peers, parents, and teachers.

The Benefit of SafeSpace

SafeSpace aims to tackle these issues by providing students with activities that reduce stress and foster emotional well-being. Through engaging, fun activities and a supportive environment, students will gain access to various tools that help them relax, build social skills, and improve their academic performance. Additionally, the platform strengthens relationships with parents and enhances overall social development.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make SafeSpace accessible to all schools across Morocco. By addressing the mental health challenges that students face, we aim to reduce stress, anxiety, and depression, thus improving both students' academic performance and overall well-being.

Our Mission

Our mission is to save significantly more students from severe mental health struggles every year, increase parental engagement, and raise teacher awareness. We strive to create a stronger, more supportive environment for students—one that enhances their mental health, builds positive social skills, and improves relationships with teachers and parents.
